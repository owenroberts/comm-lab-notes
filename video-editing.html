Types of cuts

  • Straight cut or cut - Abrupt cut from one shot to next
  • L cut or split edit - Video cuts to next shot before the audio, or vice versa
  • Graphic match - Matching similarly composed shots
  • Match or motion match - Matches motion or focus from one shot to the next, in order to disguise transition
  • Jump - Cutting ahead in same shot to show passage of time
  • Parallel edit - Cutting back and forth between two scenes repitively
