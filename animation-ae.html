Key commands

  • C/ - Add footage to Timeline
  • CSd - Split layer
  • j - Move to beginning of composition
  • k - Move to end of composition
  • i - Move to beginning of layer
  • o - Move to end of layer
  • b - Set time ruler beginning to playhead
  • n - Set time ruler end to playhead
  • , - Zoom out and . Zoom in
  • A/ - Fit to window
  • \ - Toggle between Timeline and Composition windows
  • CAt - Enable time remapping
